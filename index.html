What Should the Ideal AI Interview Agent Do?

Our AI interview agent evaluates candidates based on the specific role they're applying for, verifies minimum hiring criteria, and asks broad, foundational questions in the candidate's domain.

AI Interview Features

The interview UI features a human-like avatar. Because this is a video call, candidates must keep their own camera on to enable natural, face-to-face interaction. The session follows a typical structure: brief introductions, then role-specific questions plus a few general questions about the company or position.

AI Interview Agent Capabilities

The AI evaluates only fundamental Software Engineering capability. Candidates should be able to discuss their projects clearly, answer follow-up questions that probe depth of involvement, handle basic DSA queries, and demonstrate core knowledge of how software works.

Frequently asked questions

How does the AI interview agent work?

RNG Lab's AI interview agent features a human-like avatar that conducts natural video interviews. The AI evaluates fundamental Software Engineering capability, asks role-specific questions, and assesses candidates' ability to discuss projects clearly, handle DSA queries, and demonstrate core knowledge of how software works.

Who is RNG Lab for?

RNG Lab is designed for innovative tech companies that want to filter only truly qualified candidates for live interviews. It's perfect for software engineering roles, technical positions, and any scenario where you need to evaluate fundamental skills before investing time in live interviews.

Is RNG Lab free?

We're currently in development and accepting early access signups. Join our waitlist to be among the first to experience RNG Lab's AI interview agent when we launch. We'll have flexible pricing plans starting at $29/month for small teams, with enterprise solutions available for larger organizations. Contact us for custom pricing based on your specific needs.

What technology powers the AI interview agent?

RNG Lab uses a sophisticated technology stack: Whisper-ctranslate2 for speech recognition, vLLM + Mistral 7B for conversation processing, Piper TTS for text-to-speech, SadTalker for frame generation, and WebRTC for streaming. The system also includes OpenFace 3.0 for sentiment analysis and emotion detection.

What roles can the AI interview agent evaluate?

RNG Lab's AI interview agent is designed to evaluate fundamental Software Engineering capability for various technical roles. It can assess candidates for software engineering positions, frontend/backend development, full-stack roles, and other technical positions where fundamental programming knowledge and problem-solving skills are essential.

Can I talk to customer support?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Our dedicated support team includes technical experts who can help with setup, integration, and troubleshooting. Enterprise customers get dedicated account managers for personalized support.
