Frequently asked questions

How does the anti-cheating technology work?

RNG Luxe uses advanced AI monitoring including screen recording, eye tracking, tab switching detection, and background application monitoring. Our system analyzes candidate behavior patterns in real-time and flags suspicious activities like looking away from screen, using external devices, or accessing unauthorized resources during assessments.

Who is RNG Luxe for?

RNG Luxe is designed for HR departments, recruitment agencies, educational institutions, and any organization conducting remote assessments. It's perfect for technical interviews, coding challenges, certification exams, and any scenario where assessment integrity is crucial.

Is RNG Luxe free?

We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, we have flexible pricing plans starting at $29/month for small teams, with enterprise solutions available for larger organizations. Contact us for custom pricing based on your specific needs.

How is it invisible in assessments?

RNG Luxe operates as a background service that doesn't appear in meeting participants, screen recordings, or shared screens. It uses advanced stealth technology to monitor without detection, ensuring candidates can't identify or disable the monitoring system during assessments.

What types of assessments are supported?

RNG Luxe supports all major assessment types including coding challenges, technical tests, multiple-choice exams, video interviews, and live coding sessions. It integrates with popular platforms like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and works with custom assessment tools through our API.

Can I talk to customer support?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Our dedicated support team includes technical experts who can help with setup, integration, and troubleshooting. Enterprise customers get dedicated account managers for personalized support.
